Madi Leigh

  • Photography has always been a passion of mine, and I could not imagine where my life would be without photography. I love when I am given the honor to photograph somebody and get to create memories for them that will last a lifetime. Since I can’t imagine my life without art and photography, I am studying art and design in college to expand my knowledge and abilities behind the camera.

  • Traveling is something that has always caught my interest, which led me to studying abroad for my junior year of college in Italy. Traveling has done wonders; living in another culture quickly broadened my worldviews and outlook on life. Travel is something that I hope I can continue doing so I can really see what’s out there and experience the world. There is so much out there to see and to photograph!
